# Privacy Policy — Karaoke Voice Recorder

**Publisher:** PlayMine
**Contact:** playmine.support@gmail.com
**Last updated:** May 9, 2026

This privacy policy explains how the **Karaoke Voice Recorder** Android app handles your data. The short version: the app records audio on your device only, never sends anything to us, and never sends anything to a third party.

## 1. What the app does

Karaoke Voice Recorder lets you record your voice, optionally mixed with music, in three modes:

- **Mic Only** — captures audio from the device microphone.
- **My Audio + Voice** — plays a music file you pick from your phone and records your voice over it.
- **App Audio + Voice** — captures audio playing in another app you choose (browser, music player, etc.) and mixes it with your voice. This requires your explicit per-session consent through Android's "Share screen / app audio" system dialog.

Recordings can be played back, renamed, shared with apps you choose, or deleted, all from inside the app.

## 2. Data the app collects

The app collects only what is necessary to provide its features. All data stays on your device.

- **Audio recordings.** Created by you when you press Record. Stored in the app's private folder on your device. Not uploaded to us or to any third party. Deleted automatically when you uninstall the app, or manually when you delete a recording.
- **App settings.** Your chosen recording format (MP3 / WAV), silence-detection preferences, voice and music volume, theme, and similar in-app settings. Stored on the device only.
- **Selected audio file URI.** When you pick a music file in **My Audio + Voice**, Android grants the app permission to read that one file. The app remembers the URI so it can re-open the file across sessions. The file itself is never copied or uploaded.

The app does not collect or transmit your name, email address, phone number, contacts, location, photos, advertising ID, or any other personal identifier.

## 3. Permissions the app requests

| Permission | Why |
|---|---|
| `RECORD_AUDIO` | To record from the microphone. Required for the core feature. |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE` and `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MICROPHONE` | To keep recording reliably while the screen is off or you switch apps. |
| `FOREGROUND_SERVICE_MEDIA_PROJECTION` | Used only in **App Audio + Voice** mode, after you grant the per-session "Share" consent, to capture audio from the app you chose. |
| `POST_NOTIFICATIONS` | To show the "Recording in progress" status notification. |
| `WAKE_LOCK` | To keep the CPU awake during a recording so audio is not interrupted. |

The app does not request access to storage, photos, contacts, location, phone call state, the internet, or your accounts.

## 4. Data sharing

The app does not share data with any third party. The app does not use analytics SDKs, crash reporting SDKs, or advertising SDKs. The only outbound traffic generated by installing this app is Google Play's own update check, performed by the Google Play app on your device — that is not under our control and is governed by [Google's Play Services privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You may, on your own initiative, share an individual recording with another app (email, messaging, cloud storage, etc.) using the in-app **Share** action. In that case the receiving app handles the file according to its own privacy policy, not ours.

## 5. Data retention

Recordings remain on your device until you delete them in-app or uninstall the app. Uninstalling the app removes all recordings and settings.

## 6. Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. The app does not collect personal information from any user, including children. If you believe a child has been provided with personal information through this app, please contact us so we can investigate.

## 7. Security

Recordings live in the app's private storage area, which other apps on the device cannot read on a non-rooted device. The app does not encrypt recordings beyond what the Android platform provides at the filesystem level. Because nothing is transmitted off the device, there is no in-transit data to secure.

## 8. Your choices

- **Delete a recording**: long-press it in the Library and choose Delete.
- **Stop using the app**: uninstall it. All recordings and settings are removed.
- **Revoke microphone access**: Settings → Apps → Karaoke Voice Recorder → Permissions → Microphone → Don't allow. The app will not be able to record until you re-grant permission.
